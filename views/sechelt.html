Overview

A Customized Wordpress website for the Town of Sechelt's Visitor Centre. The visitors will get to know the town more and discover events and activities they can do in the "Land between Two Waters".

View it Here!

Objective

The Town of Sechelt is looking to have a website for their Visitor Centre that will invite newcomers to discover more about Sechelt. The site has to be friendly and inviting and should be able to showcase different events/activities in Sechelt.

Solution

I utilized white spaces and airy typography to really get that "inviting" look. The homepage has a full-width carousel which links to different pages. The carousel has a blue overlay to reflect "water". The overall color scheme came from their friendly logo.The images are bright and friendly and showcases events, community, and nature.

In Conclusion

I utilized the white spaces to have that "inviting" feel and really let the guests know that there are a lot of things to do at Sechelt.
